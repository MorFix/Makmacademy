This exercise expands the first exercise. Your simple webpage, will now transform into an email client website!

  • Your toolbar should now contain a logo image and a title.
    • You can download the image here
  • The navigation panel will have 2 available options:
  • Inbox
  • Outgoing Mail
    • Your content will contain a list of mails, each with a subject, sender name, and a preview and a mail importance.
  • A new mail button will be added
